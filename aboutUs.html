About Us

Welcome to Willow Gardens!

Nestled in the heart of Westfield, Wisconsin, Willow Gardens is more than just a community garden‚Äîit's a shared space where neighbors come together to grow, learn, and thrive. With a focus on sustainability, education, and community connections, we provide a welcoming environment for gardeners of all experience levels.

Our Mission

At Willow Gardens, our mission is to:

  • Foster a sense of community by bringing people together through gardening.
  • Promote sustainable gardening practices and education.
  • Provide access to fresh, homegrown produce for all members of the community.

What we offer

There's lots going on here, check it out!

  • Garden Plots: Rent a plot and grow your own fruits, vegetables, and flowers.
  • Volunteer Opportunities: Join our team and help make a difference in our community.
  • Workshops and Events: Learn new gardening skills, attend seasonal events, and share your knowledge with others.
  • Resources: Access tools, water, compost, and expert advice to support your gardening journey.
